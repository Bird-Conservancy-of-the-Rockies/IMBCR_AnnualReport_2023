9.1 BLM Colorado

BLM in Colorado: Total

We obtained results for BLM in Colorado: Total by compiling and jointly analyzing data from 13 strata.

Field technicians completed 90 of 92 planned surveys (98%) in 2023. Technicians conducted 997 point counts within the 90 surveyed grid cells between May 16 and July 19. They detected 144 bird species, including 22 priority species.

Bird Conservancy estimated densities and population sizes for 134 species that were detected in any year during which surveys were conducted, 19 of which are priority species. The data yielded robust density estimates (CV < 50%) for 82 species.

Bird Conservancy estimated the proportion of 1 km² grid cells occupied (Ψ, Psi) throughout BLM in Colorado: Total for 134 species that were detected in any year during which surveys were conducted, 19 of which are priority species. The data yielded robust occupancy estimates (CV < 50%) for 84 species.

View results on the Rocky Mountain Avian Data Center
